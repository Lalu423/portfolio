About Me

Hello, my name is Jonathan Lalu. I have a degree in Digital Media Production. I have been working with various types of medium over the years. I have worked with video production and graphic design. I started working with wedding videography and have worked hundreds of weddings, it never gets old for me. I have also done graphic design work for a variety of local bands in my area. I have designed flyers, T-shirts and album covers. I am always looking to expand my knowledge and expertise in the visual world.

I am currently enrolled in the UPenn coding bootcamp and looking to learn the language of HTML, CSS and Javascript. I am very excited for the experience because I know it will challenge me. I am looking forward to learn and see the things I can create with my new skill set.
